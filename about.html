About Flipgame

Flipgame is a fun and engaging memory game where you test your ability to match pairs of cards. It’s perfect for players of all ages and helps improve memory and concentration. The game features different difficulty levels, ranging from easy to master, ensuring that there’s a challenge for everyone!

How to Play:

To play Flipgame, you simply flip cards and try to find matching pairs. The game is timed, and you can choose from several levels of difficulty. As you progress, the game becomes more challenging, making it exciting for players who enjoy memory-based puzzles!

Features:

  • Multiple difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard, Master)
  • Timer-based gameplay for added challenge
  • Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Fun and colorful interface
